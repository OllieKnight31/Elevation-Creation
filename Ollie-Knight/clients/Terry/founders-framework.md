## Mechanism Summary

**Name:** The 3C System
**Type:** Transubstantiated (leveraged finance principles from private equity, repackaged for individual Airbnb entrepreneurs)
**Core Insight:** "You don't need money to build wealth. You need knowledge, credibility, and conviction. Capital follows conviction." The entire property education industry teaches "save money → invest your own capital." Terry's 3C System flips this — build credibility first, raise capital from others, then deploy it into cash-flowing Airbnb properties.

**How It Works (Broad Strokes):**

1. **Phase 1: Credibility** — Build your personal brand, leverage past wins and transferable skills, create a social media presence. Before asking anyone for money, you become someone worth investing in. This is the phase most coaches skip entirely.

2. **Phase 2: Capital** — Use your credibility to raise investment from your warm network (not cold outreach). Two models: equity model (investor funds 100%, you split profits) or debt model (loan with interest). Terry raised his first £8,500 this way as a uni student.

3. **Phase 3: Cash Flow** — Deploy raised capital into Airbnb properties in high-demand locations. Prove the model with your first property. Return capital to investors. Recycle with more investors and scale. Build a team (cleaners, maintenance, staging, VA) so you work 2 hours/day.

**Why It's Different:** Every other Airbnb coach teaches "use your own savings" or "get a mortgage." The 3C System starts with credibility — because capital follows conviction, not the other way round. This means clients can start with zero of their own money, which eliminates the biggest barrier in the industry.

**The Hope Statement:** "This time will be different because you don't need to wait until you've saved enough. The 3C System means you can start now — with other people's money — and build a profitable Airbnb business within 90 days."
