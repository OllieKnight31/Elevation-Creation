# Case Study Extraction Questionnaire
## Property Labs - Terry Dwobeng

**Instructions:** Record yourself on Zoom going through each client one at a time. Spend 3-5 minutes per client. Just talk naturally — don't overthink it. We'll clean everything up after.

Do one full run-through per client before moving to the next.

---

### Section 1: Who Is This Person?

1. What's their first name? (We'll use this or change it later if needed)
2. What were they doing before they came to you? (Job, uni, situation)
3. How old are they roughly?
4. What was their financial situation when they started? (Savings, salary, debt etc.)

---

### Section 2: Where Were They Before You?

5. What was their biggest frustration or pain point before joining?
6. Had they tried anything before? (Other courses, YouTube, doing it alone?)
7. What was holding them back from starting or scaling?
8. How did they find you? (Instagram, referral, ad, word of mouth?)

---

### Section 3: What Happened Inside The Program?

9. What was the first win or breakthrough they had?
10. How long did it take them to get their first property live?
11. What part of the system helped them the most? (e.g. sourcing, landlord scripts, raising finance, investor outreach)
12. Did they face any setbacks during the program? How did they overcome them?

---

### Section 4: The Results

13. How many properties do they have now?
14. What's their monthly profit roughly?
15. Have they quit their job or reduced hours? What does their life look like now?
16. How long have they been in the program?

---

### Section 5: The Quotable Moments

17. Is there anything they've said to you that stands out? (A message, a call, a text — anything that captures the transformation)
18. If they were sitting in front of someone on the fence about joining, what would they say?
19. What's the one thing about their story that would surprise people?

---

### Section 6: Proof & Assets

20. Do you have any screenshots, messages, or photos from this client we can use? (Airbnb listings, profit screenshots, WhatsApp messages, before/after)
21. Would this client be open to doing a short video testimonial or written quote?

---

**After recording:** Send the Zoom recording to Ollie. We'll transcribe it and pull out the case studies, ad angles, lead magnet content, and webinar proof segments from there.

Aim for at least 4-5 clients if you can. The more variety the better — try to cover:
- A complete beginner who got their first property
- Someone who was stuck and broke through
- Someone who's scaled to multiple properties
- Someone who's quit their job or gone part-time
