## 2. 10 WINNING AD PATTERNS

### Pattern 1: The Income-Per-Deal Hook
**Example:** "Get Paid £3K-£5K Per Deal Without Investing a Penny" (Samuel Leeds — running since 29 Jan, 2+ months)
**Why it works:** Specific income figure + removes the biggest objection (capital) in the headline
**Format:** Talking head video
**CTA:** Free training sign-up

### Pattern 2: The Anti-Conventional-Wisdom Hook
**Example:** "If your plan is 'work harder and save more,' you are going to hate this video." (Samuel Leeds — running since 25 Mar)
**Why it works:** Pattern interrupt that challenges the prospect's current strategy, creating curiosity gap
**Format:** Talking head video
**CTA:** Free online training

### Pattern 3: The Urgency/Fear-of-Missing-Out Hook
**Example:** "Stop scrolling. If you do not own property in 2026, you are falling behind." (Samuel Leeds — running since 22 Mar)
**Why it works:** Commands attention ("stop scrolling"), creates urgency with current year reference
**Format:** Talking head video
**CTA:** "FREE PROPERTY TRAINING NOW - Limited spots"

### Pattern 4: The Anti-Guru/Underdog Positioning
**Example:** "Started with £5K. No silver spoon. No shortcuts. Just vision, pressure, and execution." (Freeman Richards — running since 25 Mar)
**Why it works:** Contrasts with flashy guru positioning, builds authentic trust
**Format:** Raw talking head video (phone quality)
**CTA:** Instagram DM

### Pattern 5: The Controversy/Heat Hook
**Example:** "I've been catching a lot of heat recently for saying you can make six figures from one property." (CDN Coaching — running since 23 Mar)
**Why it works:** Creates curiosity through implied controversy, bold claim that demands proof
**Format:** Talking head video with property showcase
**CTA:** Visit Instagram

### Pattern 6: The Capital Objection Crusher
**Example:** "You want to get into property, but you do not have £30,000 saved. That is where rent-to-rent changes the game." (CDN Coaching — running since 26 Mar)
**Why it works:** Acknowledges exact objection the prospect has, then introduces the mechanism as the solution
**Format:** Talking head video
**CTA:** Visit Instagram

### Pattern 7: The Social Proof/Results Parade
**Example:** "Proof it works --> My mentees are securing their first Airbnb deals within weeks, not years." (Freeman Richards — running since 18 Mar)
**Why it works:** Third-party proof is more believable than self-claims, "weeks not years" compresses timeframe
**Format:** Talking head + student result clips
**CTA:** DM "AIRBNB"

### Pattern 8: The Lead Magnet Download
**Example:** "Choosing a location is half the deal. This guide shows 10 easier areas..." (Samuel Leeds — running since 20 Mar)
**Why it works:** Provides immediate specific value, lower commitment than webinar
**Format:** Static image or talking head
**CTA:** "Download the Free Guide"

### Pattern 9: The Origin Story/Transformation
**Example:** "Beth went from knowing nothing about property as an Amazon delivery driver to building a £1.3 million portfolio..." (Paul Smith Wealth)
**Why it works:** Relatable starting point, dramatic transformation, "if she can do it" psychology
**Format:** Talking head video with B-roll
**CTA:** Learn more

### Pattern 10: The "This Is Not What You Think" Reframe
**Example:** "UK property (no money down) isn't about buying. It's about finding deals others want." (Samuel Leeds — running since 30 Mar)
**Why it works:** Challenges assumption, then introduces a new frame that feels like insider knowledge
**Format:** Talking head video
**CTA:** Free training

## 3. THREE DOMINANT MESSAGING ANGLES

### Angle 1: "No Capital Required" / Low Barrier to Entry
**Used by:** Samuel Leeds, CDN Coaching, Freeman Richards, trutheceo
**Core message:** You don't need £30K+ savings, a mortgage, or property ownership to make money in property. Rent-to-rent / deal sourcing / SA lets you start with £5K-£10K.
**Frequency:** Appears in approximately 60-70% of all ads in this niche
**Risk for Terry:** This is the most saturated angle. Per Terry's brief, avoid leading with "no money" — it attracts the wrong audience.

### Angle 2: "Escape the 9-5" / Replace Your Income
**Used by:** Freeman Richards, CDN Coaching, multiple smaller coaches
**Core message:** You're stuck in a job you hate. Property (specifically SA/Airbnb) is the vehicle to replace your salary and achieve time freedom.
**Frequency:** Appears in approximately 40-50% of ads
**Risk for Terry:** Overused and attracts dreamers who may not have the budget or commitment for a high-ticket programme.

### Angle 3: "Proven System / Fast Results"
**Used by:** Freeman Richards, CDN Coaching, Samuel Leeds
**Core message:** Follow a proven system and get your first deal done in weeks, not years. Mentorship/coaching accelerates the timeline vs. figuring it out alone.
**Frequency:** Appears in approximately 30-40% of ads
**Opportunity for Terry:** This angle works but needs differentiation in the MECHANISM (what makes Terry's system different from the 50 other "proven systems").

## 4. WHITE SPACE OBSERVATIONS (What Nobody Is Saying)

### White Space 1: Nobody Is Talking About RESULTS-LED Positioning With Proof of Scale
Every competitor is either:
- Selling the dream of "your first deal" (beginner-focused)
- Using vague income claims ("six figures from one property")

**Nobody is showing a PORTFOLIO of running SA properties with actual revenue dashboards, occupancy rates, and year-over-year growth.** Terry's Approach 5 (Results-Led) could own this space by showing his actual Property Labs portfolio performance — not just "I did a deal" but "here's my business generating X per month across Y properties, and here's the system that runs it."

### White Space 2: Nobody Is Addressing Operational Reality
All competitors focus on GETTING the deal. None address:
- How to actually OPERATE properties profitably long-term
- Guest management, pricing strategy, cleaning logistics, maintenance
- What happens after month 3 when the novelty wears off
- How to scale from 1 to 5 to 10+ properties without burning out

**Terry could position his webinar around "the part nobody tells you about" — the operations/systems side that determines whether you actually keep the money you make.**

### White Space 3: Nobody Is Using Authority/Credibility Differentiation
All competitors look the same:
- Young person talking to camera
- Generic "I started with nothing" story
- Instagram DM funnels

**Nobody has corporate/professional credibility markers.** Terry could differentiate with:
- Professional production quality (not looking like every other Instagram coach)
- Client portfolio tours showing REAL properties
- Business metrics and data (not just "6 figures" claims)
- Partnership/brand association credibility

## 5. TOP 5 PROVEN HOOK FORMULAS

### Formula 1: [Specific Income Figure] + [Objection Removal]
**Template:** "Get Paid [£X-£Y] Per [Unit] Without [Biggest Objection]"
**Example:** "Get Paid £3K-£5K Per Deal Without Investing a Penny"
**Why it dominates:** Combines greed motivation with fear/objection removal in one line
**Used by:** Samuel Leeds (longest-running ad — proven winner)

### Formula 2: "If your plan is [common approach], you are going to [negative consequence]"
**Template:** "If your plan is [what they're currently doing], you are going to [hate/regret] this [video/training]"
**Example:** "If your plan is 'work harder and save more,' you are going to hate this video."
**Why it dominates:** Pattern interrupt + curiosity gap + challenges current strategy
**Used by:** Samuel Leeds

### Formula 3: "Stop scrolling. If you [don't have X] in [current year], you are [falling behind]"
**Template:** "Stop scrolling. If you [haven't done X] in [year], you are [consequence]."
**Example:** "Stop scrolling. If you do not own property in 2026, you are falling behind."
**Why it dominates:** Commands attention + time pressure + status anxiety
**Used by:** Samuel Leeds

### Formula 4: "You want [desired outcome], but you [don't have prerequisite]. That is where [mechanism] changes the game."
**Template:** "You want to [goal], but you do not have [common barrier]. That is where [your mechanism] changes the game."
**Example:** "You want to get into property, but you do not have £30,000 saved. That is where rent-to-rent changes the game."
**Why it dominates:** Empathy + mechanism introduction — the "I see you, and I have the answer" structure
**Used by:** CDN Coaching

### Formula 5: "[Proof signal] --> My [clients/mentees] are [achieving result] within [short timeframe], not [long timeframe]."
**Template:** "Proof it works --> My [students] are [specific result] within [weeks], not [years]."
**Example:** "Proof it works --> My mentees are securing their first Airbnb deals within weeks, not years."
**Why it dominates:** Social proof + compressed timeframe + implied contrast with doing it alone
**Used by:** Freeman Richards

## 6. TALKING HEAD VIDEO PATTERNS

### Production Quality Spectrum

**High Production (Samuel Leeds):**
- Professional lighting
- Clean background (sometimes office, sometimes property)
- Good audio quality
- Edited with cuts, B-roll of properties
- Thumbnail has text overlay
- Multiple versions tested per creative
- Landscape and square formats

**Mid Production (CDN Coaching):**
- Decent camera quality (possibly iPhone with good lighting)
- Casual setting but intentional
- Property walkthroughs mixed in
- Text overlays showing income figures
- Vertical format (Instagram-optimised)

**Raw/Authentic (Freeman Richards):**
- Phone camera quality
- Feels like organic Instagram content repurposed as ads
- Very casual — could be an Instagram story
- Vertical format only
- No fancy editing — just cuts
- This style builds trust through "realness"

### Common Elements Across All Winning Videos:
1. **Direct eye contact to camera** — always talking TO the viewer, not about them
2. **Start talking immediately** — no intros, no logos, no "hey guys" — straight into the hook
3. **First 3 seconds is the hook line** — the opening text matches the video's opening words
4. **Walking/moving format** — several ads show the person walking through properties while talking
5. **Income proof on screen** — text overlays showing figures, screenshots of bookings/revenue
6. **Length: 60-180 seconds** — no ads over 3 minutes detected in top positions
7. **Subtitles/captions** — most videos have burned-in subtitles (critical for sound-off viewing)

### What's NOT Seen:
- No whiteboard presentations
- No slideshows or PowerPoint-style ads
- No animated explainer videos
- No stock footage
- No "guru on stage" clips
- Very few static image ads (Samuel Leeds has one, but it's the exception)

## 7. STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS FOR TERRY

### Based on This Research, Terry's Webinar Ads Should:

**1. AVOID the "no money needed" angle entirely.**
Every competitor leads with this. It attracts tyre-kickers. Terry's Results-Led approach (Approach 5) is already the correct differentiation.

**2. Lead with RESULTS and OPERATIONAL PROOF.**
White Space 1 is the biggest opportunity. Show actual portfolio performance — revenue dashboards, occupancy rates, before/after property transformations, team operations. This is what nobody else is doing.

**3. Use the "You want X but don't have Y — that's where [mechanism] comes in" hook formula.**
This is the most adaptable for Terry's audience. Example:
- "You've watched 100 YouTube videos about serviced accommodation. You still don't have a single booking. That's because nobody taught you the OPERATIONS side."

**4. Produce mid-to-high quality talking head videos.**
Not raw iPhone footage (that's Freeman Richards' lane). Not overly polished (that feels corporate). The sweet spot is professional but personal — good lighting, clean background, casual delivery, property B-roll.

**5. Drive to WEBINAR REGISTRATION, not Instagram DMs.**
Samuel Leeds (the biggest spender and most sophisticated) drives to webinar/training registration. Freeman Richards and CDN Coaching drive to Instagram DMs. The webinar funnel is the higher-leverage, more scalable approach. Terry is already building this correctly.

**6. Use the "Stop [common approach], start [Terry's approach]" framework.**
Terry's differentiation is that he doesn't just teach deal-finding — he teaches the complete BUSINESS of running SA properties. The hook should position everyone else's teaching as incomplete.

**7. Test these specific hook angles for Terry:**
- "Everyone teaches you how to find your first Airbnb deal. Nobody teaches you how to keep it profitable past month 3."
- "I manage [X] serviced accommodation properties generating [£Y/month]. Here's what nobody on YouTube is telling you about this business."
- "The property coaching industry has a dirty secret: 90% of their students never make a second booking."
- "Stop watching another 'how to start Airbnb' video. Start learning how to BUILD an Airbnb BUSINESS."

**8. Long-running ad format (for testing):**
- 90-second talking head video
- Hook line as the first sentence (no warm-up)
- 2-3 proof points / results (show actual properties/revenue)
- Mechanism intro (Property Labs method)
- CTA: "Register for the free live training — [date]"
- Thumbnail: Terry's face + text overlay with result/claim
