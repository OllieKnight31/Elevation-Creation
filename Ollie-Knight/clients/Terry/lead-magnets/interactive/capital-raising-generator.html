How Ehab Raised £25k in 5 Weeks

A step-by-step breakdown of exactly what he did.

1

Week 1: Foundation

Ehab identified a 2-bed property in Birmingham, worked out setup costs (7,500 pounds), projected returns, and built his Credibility One-Pager. He started posting on Instagram -- nothing fancy, just sharing that he was learning about the Airbnb business model. He did not ask anyone for money yet. He built the foundation first.

2

Week 2: Warm Outreach

Made a list of 15 people. Sent the Warm Approach text to 8. 5 replied positively. 2 did not reply. 1 said "not for me right now." Booked coffee meetings with 3 of them that week. Focused on the people most likely to say yes first.

3

Week 3: First Meetings

Three sit-down conversations using the meeting script (practised out loud 10 times). Person 1: "let me think about it." Person 2: Yes in principle -- 10,000 pounds. Person 3: Loved it, introduced Ehab to his uncle.

4

Week 4: Social Media Kicks In

6 posts on Instagram. A mix of journey updates, education, and behind-the-scenes. Someone he had not spoken to in 2 years DM'd him. That person invested 7,500 pounds. Person 1 from Week 3 also came back and confirmed 7,500 pounds.

5

Week 5: Closed

Three confirmed investors. Former colleague: 7,500 pounds. Family friend: 10,000 pounds. Instagram contact: 7,500 pounds. Total raised: 25,000 pounds. Five weeks. Zero of his own money. Both units were cash-flow positive within 45 days.

"The hardest part was sending the first text. Literally my hands were shaking. But once I got that first yes, everything changed. The scripts gave me something to actually say instead of just winging it. And people were way more receptive than I expected -- most of them were actually grateful I brought it to them because they were bored of their savings account giving them nothing."
-- Ehab, Property Labs Student
