### Testing Framework

**Week 1-2. Hook Testing**
- Launch 6-8 cold video ads across 2 ad sets (1 broad, 1 interest-stacked)
- Each video = different hook, same body/CTA structure
- Launch 3-4 static ads alongside (70% budget video, 30% static)
- Let ads run 3-4 days minimum before decisions
- Kill any ad with CTR below 1% after £20+ spend
- Promote any ad with CTR above 2% to higher budget
- Key metric: Outbound CTR

**Week 3-4. Angle Testing**
- Take top 3 hooks from week 1-2
- Create new versions with different angle bodies (pain vs aspiration vs mechanism)
- Compare CPL across angles
- Key metric: Cost per registration

**Week 5+. Scale Winners**
- Push budget into top 2-3 creatives (lowest CPL, highest CVR)
- Increase daily budget by 20-30% every 2-3 days
- Duplicate winning ad sets into broader targeting campaigns
- Launch lookalike audiences based on registrant data
- Test 1-2 new creatives per week to prevent fatigue
- Key metric: CPL, ROAS, registration volume

**Ongoing Rules**
- Never edit a live ad. duplicate and modify the duplicate
- Replace any creative with frequency above 3.0 in retargeting
- Test new thumbnails/covers every 2 weeks

### Recording Guidelines

**Locations**
- Modern apartment (primary). talking-head, reflective, mechanism scripts
- London streets with gimbal. energetic, pain-driven, contrarian scripts
- Inside/outside Airbnb properties. social proof, aspiration scripts
- Coffee shop. story-driven, casual, shorter ads

**Lighting**
- Always face the light source (windows in front, not behind)
- Film during golden hour (7-8am or 5-7pm)
- Indoor night: desk lamp or ring light at eye level
- Avoid mixing warm bulbs with daylight
- iPhone: 4K 30fps, HDR off, lock exposure by pressing and holding on face

**Audio**
- Use a wireless lapel mic (Rode Wireless Go II or DJI Mic recommended)
- No lapel mic = hold phone at arm's length max
- Film in rooms with soft furnishings to reduce echo
- Do a 5-second test recording before every session

**Delivery**
- Hit the hook on beat one. no "Hey guys" or "So basically"
- Deliver hooks like you are telling your best mate something unbelievable
- Use your hands. gesture, point, count on fingers
- Vary pace: fast for hooks/agitation, slow for reflective moments, punch the CTA
- Record each hook 3-5 times, pick the one with the most energy
- Talk to one person. imagine FaceTiming your younger cousin

**Wardrobe**
- Default: clean fitted smart-casual (dark polo or crew-neck, slim trousers/jeans, clean trainers)
- Avoid: all-white, heavy patterns/logos, overdressed
- For authority: subtle watch or chain
- Wear similar colour palettes across same-batch videos

**Batch Filming**
- Film 5-8 scripts per session, change location/outfit midway
- Display script on a monitor behind camera at eye level (reference, not read)
- Start with highest-energy scripts while fresh
- Check playback between takes (framing, lighting, audio, energy)
- 2 filming sessions needed for all 35 video scripts (~2-3 hours each)
