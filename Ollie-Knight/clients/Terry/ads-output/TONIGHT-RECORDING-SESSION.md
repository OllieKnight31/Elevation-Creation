RECORDING TIPS:
- Film in good natural light or ring light
- Smart casual (dark polo or crew neck, no logos)
- Look directly at camera
- First 3 seconds = HIGHEST energy. This is everything.
- Do NOT read word for word. Learn the key point, then deliver naturally.
- Film 2-3 takes of each
- Vertical (9:16) for Reels/Stories placements
- Always add captions in editing (80% completion rate boost)
- Mix locations: apartment, walking outside, in front of a property

META COMPLIANCE (NEW ACCOUNT, IMPORTANT):
- Do NOT say "you" in a way that calls out personal attributes
  ("You are broke" is risky. "So why do you still feel broke?" is safer as a question)
- Do NOT make income claims or guarantees in the ad itself
  (Say "results vary" or "here is what happened for one student" not "you WILL make X")
- Do NOT use before/after transformation language about finances
- AVOID words: "guaranteed income", "get rich", "passive income" (use "property income" instead)
- Do NOT show income screenshots, bank balances, or revenue dashboards
- Keep claims factual: "14 units", "25+ students" are verifiable facts, not promises
- The webinar is FREE so there is no financial commitment. Lead with that.
- Start with SOFT ads (educational, story-led) for the first 7 days to warm the account
- Do NOT launch all 15 ads at once. Start with 3-5 of the softest ones first.
