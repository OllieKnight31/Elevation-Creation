HOW TO FILM (ORGANIC SELFIE STYLE)

These ads need to feel like you just grabbed your phone and started talking.
NOT like a polished production. The more natural it looks, the better it performs.

Camera:
Use your iPhone front camera (selfie mode). Hold it slightly above eye level for a more flattering angle. Keep your face in the top third of the frame. Arms length distance, not too close.

Lighting:
Natural daylight is best. Face a window. If filming at night, use a ring light but keep it soft. No harsh overhead lighting, it creates shadows under the eyes.

Audio:
Film somewhere quiet, no echo. AirPods Pro work great as a mic if you are outside. Do NOT use background music while recording (add in editing).

Locations (mix these up across the 24 scripts):
Walking through your apartment (casual, morning energy).
Sitting on the sofa or at a desk (relaxed, conversational).
Walking through London streets (dynamic, confident energy).
Standing in front of a nice building or property (credibility).
In a cafe or coworking space (relatable, professional).
In the car before a meeting (intimate, urgent energy).

Delivery style:
Talk like you are FaceTiming a friend who asked you about property. Do NOT perform. Just talk. Look at the camera lens, not the screen. Smile naturally at the start of each hook. Use hand gestures, they make you look more natural. It is OK to stumble slightly, it makes it feel real. Film 2-3 takes of each, pick the most natural one. Keep your energy HIGH for the first 3 seconds then relax into the body.

Wardrobe:
Whatever you would normally wear on a day off. Dark crew neck, polo, or zip-up. No big logos or busy patterns. Change tops between every 5-6 scripts so it looks like different days.

Editing notes (for whoever edits):
Add captions to every video (this boosts completion by 80%). Keep the raw selfie look, do NOT add fancy transitions. Subtle background music at 10-15% volume max. Cut any dead air at the start, the hook should hit immediately. Export vertical 9:16 for Reels/Stories placements.


META COMPLIANCE (NEW ACCOUNT)

Do NOT say "you are broke" or call out personal financial attributes.
Do NOT make income guarantees ("you WILL earn X").
Say "here is what happened for one student" not "you will get these results".
Avoid "passive income" (use "property income" or "monthly income from Airbnb").
Keep claims factual: "14 units", "25+ students" are verifiable.
The webinar is FREE. Lead with that in every CTA.
No income screenshots or revenue dashboards on screen.
