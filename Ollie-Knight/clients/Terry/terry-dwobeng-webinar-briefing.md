## The 3C System (Unique Mechanism)
Terry's proprietary framework inspired by private equity leveraged finance:

1. **Credibility** — Build personal brand, leverage past wins, transferable skills, social media presence
2. **Capital** — Use credibility to raise investment from warm contacts (not cold outreach). Equity model (investor funds 100%, split profits) or debt model (loan with interest)
3. **Cash flow** — Deploy capital into Airbnb properties in high-demand locations, prove the model, return capital, recycle with more investors

**Core Insight:** "You don't need money to build wealth. You need knowledge, credibility, and conviction. Capital follows conviction."

**Old Way vs New Way:**
- Old: Save money for months/years → invest your own capital → high risk
- New: Build credibility → raise finance from warm network → in profit from day one → compound faster
